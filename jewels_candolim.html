Our Story

Candolim's Finest
Jewelry Destination

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Candolim — near Newton's Supermarket, opposite the famous Elephant Shop — Jewels of India is Goa's most beloved fine jewelry boutique. We bring the grandest traditions of Indian goldsmithing directly to the streets of North Goa.

Our Candolim store is a destination in itself. Tourists, travelers, and locals alike step in to discover handcrafted pieces spanning Kundan, Polki, Meenakari, Temple jewelry, and contemporary diamond designs — all under one beautifully curated roof.

When you carry a piece from Jewels of India, you carry a fragment of India's timeless legacy.

Where Goa Meets Grandeur

A Store as Beautiful
as the Jewels Inside

Candolim is one of Goa's most iconic beach destinations — and our store sits right at its cultural pulse. The moment you step inside, you're transported from the sun-soaked streets into a world of golden light, precious stones, and timeless craftsmanship.

Our curated boutique experience means you're never just shopping — you're discovering stories. Each piece is presented with its heritage, technique, and artisan lineage. Our knowledgeable staff guide you through every tradition.

Whether you're a tourist looking for a memorable keepsake or a collector seeking rare pieces, Jewels of India Candolim is your destination.
